THE POSTULANCY STAGE

is the first stage in our formation programe,
its aim is to introduce you to our society and our live as pallottines. it last for about 10-12months after the postulancy programe and one comes out successful, he will be admitted into the Novitiate

THE NOVITIATE STAGE

the novitiate is where you learn about the charism and spostolate of our society, the very core of our life as pallottines.
you get intorduce to the patrimony left to us by our saintly founder; Vincent pallotti. it last for about 2year; one canonical year inside the novitiate house and one apostolic year outside the novitiate.
upon complition of this programe, one make his first consecration and those for priesthood proceed to study philosophy and theology before becoming ordianed.
while those for brotherhood are posted to an apostolate.
